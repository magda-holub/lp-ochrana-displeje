Frequently Asked Questions

Can I purchase the service even if I buy a screen protector for my current device?

Yes, you can. Pick the glass screen protector and buy the service. Our technician will properly install the glass screen protector in the shop of your choice.

How long does it take to carry out the service from the day of order?

If you buy a glass screen protector and the service for your current device, the delivery date will depend on the selected delivery method. If you purchase a new mobile device and buy this service, the delivery time will be extended by one working day compared to the delivery method you selected.

What should I do if I am not satisfied with the service?

If the glass screen protector is incorrectly installed by our technician, we will refund the service fee and the price of the glass screen protector. In case of intentional damage or if the glass screen protector is removed, the service fee is not refundable, as you used the service and it was properly carried out.

How should I proceed with a complaint about the service?

In the event of a complaint, photographic documenting is required. Complaints can be made at any of our stores or online at Alza.hu. The complaint will then be assessed and resolved.
